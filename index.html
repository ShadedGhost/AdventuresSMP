About Adventures SMP

Welcome to Adventures SMP. We are a small Minecraft server started in 2022, founded by Mythos (our admin) and Ace (the server dev). After 2024, Mythos handed over control to Ace, who has been working tirelessly on improving the server, creating a custom launcher, mods, and fostering a close-knit community despite its small size.

What is Adventures SMP?

The Adventures SMP was originally a public server on Facebook, but it lost traction over time. After Mythos left, Ace took over and transformed the server into a private, small-scale survival multiplayer server for a tight-knit group of friends who play Minecraft, make videos, and stream. The server aims to explore Minecraft in various playstyles, from classic survival to life steal, factions, and modded experiences.

Current Goals

  • Establish a dedicated website to make the server more widely known.
  • Create a dedicated PC for the server's hosting.
  • Develop a custom launcher for easy access to modded content and community exploration of past worlds.
  • Continue creating custom mods to provide unique gameplay experiences.
  • Expand the server community and increase content production to grow awareness.

Next Season - 2025

We aim to launch the next season of Adventures SMP in the summer of 2025. Our player count is expected to be around 13 players, ranging from beginners to experienced players.
